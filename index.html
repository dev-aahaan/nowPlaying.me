About

nowPlaying.me brings you insights about your music listening habits that Spotify's yearly wrapped doesn't show you. We believe music analytics should be available year-round, not just once a year.

Our platform connects securely with your Spotify account to provide real-time statistics and visualizations about your listening patterns.
